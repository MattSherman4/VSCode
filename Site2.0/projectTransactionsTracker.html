Transactions Tracker

Track the NFL's player movement; cheat at the Immaculate Grid. This interactive treemap follows NFL transactions. What players have moved from the Packers to the Giants? What colleges do the Steelers frequent players from? What teams do the Titans typically acquire players from? All these answers and more in this Plotly based app. Data available form 2000 - 2023.

About
Incspired by the Immaculate Grid game, this project's purpose is to discover which teams often share players and uncover trends in player movement. Pair this information with staff changes to better predict the offseason and gain an advantage in player negotiations and the draft.
